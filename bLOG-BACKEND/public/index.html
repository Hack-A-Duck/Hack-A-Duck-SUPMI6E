Packed with a combo of dedication and killer skills topped with childlike innocence, it’ll not be an exaggeration to say that Mayank has become the apple of everyone’s eyes , and his score in college tests narrate a similar story . With skills at his disposal and time in hand , Mayank decided to realise his long time dream of writing blogs , and this website caters to this very purpose . Mayank intends to pen down an amalgam of topics ranging from science to sarcasm , from nature to politics and what not . So let’s begin with our journey of witnessing the world through Mayank’s eyes and coming out refreshed each time we leave this site , awestruck at his views and equally perplexed at the soothing simplicity of his writing .
